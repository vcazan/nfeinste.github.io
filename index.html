Who?

A quirky Jew from Thornhill, ON Jessica is a an actor, singer/songwriter, and creator. Currently living in Toronto, Jessica loves yoga, playing her ukulele and writing poetry. Jessica is currently in her Third Year in Sheridan College's Prestigious Bachelor of Music Theatre Performance. For more info, check out the rest of this site!.
